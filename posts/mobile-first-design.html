Top 5 NFL Quarterbacks to Watch This Season

The signal-callers who will define the year.

Published: October 26, 2025 • 8 min read

The NFL is a quarterback-driven league. If you have a star under center, you have a chance. If you don't, you're looking at a long season. As we approach the 2025 season, several QBs are under the microscope. Some are legends looking for one last ride, while others are young phenoms ready to take the next step.

1. The Reigning King

Coming off another MVP season, the expectations are sky-high. With a revamped receiving corps and an offensive line that has only gotten stronger, there is no reason to believe he won't dominate again. The key will be staying healthy and navigating a brutal division schedule.

2. The Young Gun

After a sensational rookie year, the sophomore slump is the only concern. However, his poise in the pocket and ability to extend plays suggests he is the real deal. Watch for him to take more command of the offense and push the ball downfield more aggressively this year.

3. The Comeback Kid

Returning from a major injury is never easy, but reports from training camp are glowing. If he can return to his pre-injury form, his team instantly becomes a Super Bowl contender. The mobility might be limited early on, so expect a more pocket-focused approach.

Conclusion

The quarterback battle is always the most fascinating storyline of the NFL season. Keep an eye on these three, plus the rest of the field, as they vie for gridiron glory.
